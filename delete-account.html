Delete Account Request

Last updated: June 2026

BraveHR users may request deletion of their account and associated personal data by contacting us.

How to request account deletion

To request account deletion, please email us at:

hr@matesgroup.com.au

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Your full name
  • Your registered email address
  • Your employee ID, if applicable
  • A clear request to delete your BraveHR account

What data may be deleted

Upon verification of your request, we may delete or anonymize your account information, including name, email address, phone number, employee ID, and app login details.

Data we may retain

Certain attendance records may be retained for legal, payroll, employment, compliance, or business record purposes where required by law or company policy.

Processing time

We aim to process valid deletion requests within 30 days.
